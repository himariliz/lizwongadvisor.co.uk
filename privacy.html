15. Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS)

We are covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS). You may be entitled to compensation if we cannot meet our obligations.

For more information about the FSCS:

  • Website: www.fscs.org.uk
  • Phone: 0800 678 1100 or 020 7741 4100

Compensation Limits:

The FSCS provides different levels of protection depending on the type of financial product:

  • Deposits: Protected up to £120,000 per person, per institution
  • Investments: Protected up to £85,000 per person, per firm
  • Mortgage advice and arranging: Protected up to £85,000 per person, per firm
  • Insurance (compulsory): Protected for 100% of the claim
  • Insurance (non-compulsory): Protected for 90% of the claim
